About Dormcon Meetings#

DormCon Meetings are open to all dorm residents. If you are not a resident in one of MIT's eleven dormitories but would like to attend a DormCon meeting, please email us (dormcon-exec@mit.edu).

The Zoom link for our meeting can be found here.

The agenda for each meeting is sent to dormcon-announce@mit.edu two days before the meeting. Feel free to add yourself, or email dormcon-secretary@mit.edu for help.
