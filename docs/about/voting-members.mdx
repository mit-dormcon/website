# Voting Members

Each dorm represented by DormCon has an elected representative (typically the
dorm president) who acts as a DormCon voting member. For undergraduates living
in grad housing, the representative is elected at the beginning of the academic
year.

For more info, see [our constitution](/pdf/constitution.pdf).
